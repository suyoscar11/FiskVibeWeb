What is FiskVibe?

FiskVibe serves as the ultimate platform for Fisk University students, revolutionizing campus information access. By eliminating the need to switch between multiple apps, FiskVibe consolidates all the requirements—event updates, student networking, resources, dining, vital services, and much more—into one convenient hub. Effortlessly sign up, connect with like-minded peers, and let our AI chatbot assist with any inquiries—whether it's locating the right contact for your SSN, exploring new campus activities, or suggesting a unique nickname for you.
